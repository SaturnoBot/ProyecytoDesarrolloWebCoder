    Pampita is an albino cat, she was very sick and hurt when my mother found her on the street, we instantly decided to adopt her and we never regretted it.

    When she got home, at first we believed that she was not undernourished, because she was a little fat, it turns out that she was not fat, she was pregnant, and on December 24, 2019, she gave birth to two beautiful babies.

    We take care of the babies until they have lived long enough not to depend on their mother no more, and we look for a home for each one of them, where they now live happily. Now Pampi is about 2 years old, she follows me everywhere, she is very healthy, loving and playful.
